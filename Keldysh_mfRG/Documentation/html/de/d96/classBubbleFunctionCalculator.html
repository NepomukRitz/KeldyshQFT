Class that actually performs the bubble computation. Invoked by the bubble_function.

Template Parameters
channelTwo-particle channel of the computation. Can be a, p, or t.
QTemplate parameter specifying the type of the data.
vertexType_resultType of the vertex that results in the bubble computation.
vertexType_leftType of the vertex that enters as the left part of the computation.
vertexType_rightType of the vertex that enters as the right part of the computation.
Bubble_ObjectType of the Bubble object to connect the two vertices
